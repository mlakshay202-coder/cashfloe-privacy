CashFloe

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

Axay Labs ("we," "our," or "us") built CashFloe as a mobile application for personal finance and cash book management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the CashFloe app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name and phone number for authentication purposes. Phone number verification is handled through Firebase Authentication.

Financial Data: The app stores transaction data you enter, including amounts, party names, payment modes, categories, dates, and notes. This data is stored to provide the cash book functionality you use the app for.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and performance improvement.

SMS Data (Optional, Premium Feature): If you enable the SMS auto-import feature, the app reads transaction-related SMS messages from your device to automatically create transaction entries. SMS content is processed locally on your device and only extracted transaction data (amount, party name, date) is stored. We do not store or transmit raw SMS content to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the cash book and transaction tracking features
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Sync your data across devices in real-time
  • Generate financial reports (PDF and Excel) as requested by you
  • Display analytics and spending insights based on your transaction data
  • Enable team collaboration features (sharing cash books with members you invite)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in the Asia South 1 (Mumbai) region. Firebase provides enterprise-grade security including encryption in transit and at rest.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your financial data is yours.

We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With your consent: When you share a cash book with other members using invite codes, those members can see the transaction data in that specific cash book based on their assigned role.
  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Google AdMob for displaying advertisements to free-tier users. These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Advertisements

The free version of CashFloe displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can remove ads by subscribing to a premium plan. For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your transaction data is visible within the app at all times.
  • Export your data: You can generate and download PDF and Excel reports of your transactions.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual transactions, entire cash books, or your account. Contact us at the email below for full account deletion.
  • Withdraw consent: You can disable SMS auto-import at any time from the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

CashFloe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
